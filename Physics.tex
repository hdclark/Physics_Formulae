\section{SI Base Units}

\textbf{Length} \sfrac{L}{metre}: path length of light in vacuum during 
$dt=\sfrac{1}{299792458}s$. 
\textbf{Mass} \sfrac{M}{kilogram}: mass of an international prototype.
\textbf{Time} \sfrac{T}{second}: $dt$ of $9192631770$ $^{133}Cs$ 
(hyperfine split, $@\,0\,K$) ground state oscillations.
\textbf{Elec.\ Current} \sfrac{I}{ampere}: produces $\SI{2E-7}{\sfrac{N}{m}}$
between two $L=\infty$, $A=0$ wires $1\,m$ apart in vaccum.
\textbf{Thermo.\ Temp.} \sfrac{$\Theta$}{kelvin}:
\sfrac{1}{273.16}\textsuperscript{th} the $T$ of triple point water.
\textbf{Amount of Substance} \sfrac{N}{mole}: as many entities (atoms,
molecules, $e^{-}$, \etc) as there are atoms in $12\,g$ of ground state $^{12}C$.
\textbf{Luminous Intensity} \sfrac{J}{candela}: directional intensity of 
a monochromatic ($f=\SI{5.40E12}{Hz}$) source with directional radiant intensity
\sfrac{1}{683} watt per steradian.
